Other online resources

 

An integral part of the DSBK program is to foster curiosity in our Universe. Since our volunteers cannot always be around to answer questions, here is a sample of our favorite online resources for K-4 education:

  • Astronomy Picture of the Day - Stunning daily images of the Universe
  • NASA Kids Club - Fun activities for kids generated by NASA
  • NASA for K-4 Education - Activities designed for the K-4 level
  • NASA for Kids: The Solar System - Activities for kids covering our solar system
  • NASA Space Place - Astronomy games for kids
  • Star Child - Become a young astronomer and explore our Universe
  • IYA2009: Cosmic Diary - Journal entries that explore the exciting lives of Astronomers across the world
  • IAU: 100 Hours of Astronomy - An IAU event that brought observers together from around the world.
  • 365 Days of Astronomy - A daily 10-minute podcast about astronomy from people all over the world. Look for numerous contributions from UVA and Charlottesville astronomers. Listen to a special DSBK episode from March 30, 2013!

Outreach and Education

 

DSBK presents updates to the scientific community each year, about new curriculum, developing collaborations, and major achievements, at the biannual meetings of the American Astronomical Society. These presentations allow us to reflect on the past year, and introduce plans and ideas for the coming years!
