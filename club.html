After-School Club


The backbone of the DSBK program is our two-month Astronomy Club which we host at a different elementary school each semester. This long-term commitment enables the volunteers to build meaningful relationships with the students and take learning to a deeper level than is possible through isolated events alone. Our semester-long program is designed to lay the foundation for a life-long interest in science.
