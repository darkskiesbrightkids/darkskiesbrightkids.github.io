Come explore the universe with the Dark Skies, Bright Kids Summer Camp!

 

Our summer camp is open to all students who will enter 4th, 5th, or 6th grade in Fall 2022, and who are excited to learn about rockets, stars, galaxies, and the Universe. No science background is required; we're here to teach you!

 

At camp, you'll have the opportunity to

  • explore the night sky
  • visit other planets using the planetarium
  • learn about constellations, stars, and galaxies
  • make your own comets
  • discover how scientists use "invisible light"
  • work to solve the mysteries of the universe!
At the end, we will host a Star Party where you can look through telescopes and enjoy the night sky!
